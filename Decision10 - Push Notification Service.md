# Push Notification Service

## Status
Proposed

## Context

Engaging users with order updates, new product arrivals, and exclusive offers is key for user retention and to drive sales. A reliable and effective push notification service is needed to deliver timely notifications to users.

## Decision

Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)

## Consequences/Rationale

FCM integrates well with Firebase Analytics, is cost-effective, and provides robust features for delivering notifications to both Android and iOS devices.
